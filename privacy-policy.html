Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Overview

LoopTube A-B is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

Data Collection

The Extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What Data is Processed Locally

The Extension processes the following data locally on your device only:

  • YouTube video IDs (public identifiers from video URLs)
  • Timestamp positions for points A and B
  • Loop mode preferences (loop, once, or none)

How Data is Stored

All data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Data never leaves your computer
  • Data is not synchronized across devices
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage: Store A-B loop points and preferences locally
  • host_permissions (YouTube): Inject controls into YouTube video player

Content Scripts

The Extension injects content scripts on YouTube pages to add A-B loop controls to the video player. Content scripts do NOT read page content, track browsing, or collect personal information.

Data Sharing

The Extension does NOT share data with anyone.

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party services
  • No ads or monetization

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No transmission of data over the internet
  • No backend servers or databases
  • No cloud storage

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: You can view all stored data through the Extension
  • Delete: Uninstall the Extension to delete all data
  • Control: You have full control over your data at all times

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Click "Clear" button in the Extension to remove A-B points for current video
  2. Uninstall the Extension to delete all stored data

Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, advertising networks, or social media integration.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Compliance

This Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Privacy Policy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Your Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to this privacy policy.

This extension is not affiliated with YouTube or Google LLC.
